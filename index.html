Squid Game Candy

What is dalgona candy? If dalgona candy sounds familiar it’s probably because you might have heard of the viral dalgona coffee sensation last year? Everyone and their grandmas made it! Dalgona candy is an old school Korean honeycomb candy that’s similar to seafoam or honeycomb toffee or the inside of a Crunchie bar. It’s sweet and crunchy and delicious. In Korea, street vendors sell dalgona (also known as ppopgi) stamped with shapes. If you manage to eat around the candy keeping the shape intact, sometimes they give you a free candy! Of course in Squid Game it’s much more deadly.
